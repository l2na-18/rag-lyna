
Hunter x Hunter Arcs Summary:
1. Hunter Exam Arc: Gon, Killua, Kurapika, and Leorio meet and take the Hunter Exam.
2. Zoldyck Family Arc: Gon and friends travel to Kukuroo Mountain to bring Killua back.
3. Heavens Arena Arc: Gon and Killua learn Nen and fight Hisoka.
4. Yorknew City Arc: The Phantom Troupe (Genei Ryodan) steals artifacts and fights Kurapika.
5. Greed Island Arc: Gon and Killua enter a deadly video game created by Ging Freecss.
6. Chimera Ant Arc: A dangerous invasive species threatens humanity; Netero fights Meruem.
7. Election Arc: The Hunter Association elects a new chairman while Killua saves Alluka.

Nen Types and Groups:
- Enhancers: Increase natural abilities.
- Transmuters: Change the properties of aura.
- Emitters: Project aura.
- Conjurers: Create objects from aura.
- Manipulators: Control animate or inanimate objects.
- Specialists: Unique abilities that don't fit other categories.

Notable Groups:
- The Zodiacs: Twelve elite Hunters chosen by Chairman Netero.
- The Phantom Troupe: A world-renowned criminal organization led by Chrollo Lucilfer.
- The Zoldyck Family: A family of elite assassins.
- Hunter Association: The governing body of Hunters.
