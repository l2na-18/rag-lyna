Hunter x Hunter General Knowledge Base

Overview:
Hunter x Hunter is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi. The story focuses on a young boy named Gon Freecss who discovers that his father, who left him at a young age, is actually a world-renowned Hunter, a licensed professional who specializes in fantastical pursuits such as locating rare or unidentified animal species, treasure hunting, surveying unexplored enclaves, or hunting down lawless individuals.

Main Characters:
1. Gon Freecss: An athletic, rustic, and friendly boy who is searching for his father, Ging Freecss. His Nen affinity is Enhancement. He uses a fishing rod as a weapon early on, and later relies on his Jajanken technique.
2. Killua Zoldyck: A child from the Zoldyck Family of assassins. He ran away from home to become a Rookie Hunter. His Nen affinity is Transmutation. He can transmute his aura into electricity.
3. Kurapika: The sole survivor of the Kurta Clan, a race with eyes that turn scarlet in times of anger or emotional agitation. He aims to avenge his clan and recover their stolen eyes. His Nen affinity is Conjuration, but when his eyes turn scarlet, he becomes a Specialist (Emperor Time).
4. Leorio Paradinight: A young man whose goal is to become a doctor. He fights using a switchblade and his Nen affinity is Emission.

The Hunter Exam:
The Hunter Examination is a grueling, multi-stage test that applicants must pass to become licensed Hunters. The pass rate is incredibly low, and participants risk death or serious injury. Passing grants the Hunter License, which provides incredible privileges.

Nen (Mind Force):
Nen is a technique that allows a living being to use and manipulate their own life energy (aura). There are six Nen types:
1. Enhancement: The ability to use aura to increase the natural abilities of an object or one's own body.
2. Transmutation: The ability to change the properties of one's aura to mimic something else.
3. Emission: The ability to detach aura from one's body and project it.
4. Conjuration: The ability to create a physical, independent, material object out of one's aura.
5. Manipulation: The ability to control living or non-living things with aura.
6. Specialization: Anything that does not belong to the other five categories.

Greed Island:
Greed Island is a Joystation video game created by Ging Freecss and his friends. It transports players physically into the game's world, which is actually a real island in the real world. The game relies heavily on cards and Nen.
